The Top 6 French Restaurant/Cafes here in the Philippines

by Lady Grace Cannu | March 14, 2023

Here are my top 6 restaurant and cafe recommendations that you will surely love. These restaurants/cafes are also top rating according to google

1. Brasserie Sagana

4.6      ·  French
Taguig, Metro Manila

Brasserie Sagana is a French Restaurant serving authentic, honest French cuisine in a sleek modern setting. For decades, our Chef Marc Aubry has been committed to bringing the best of France to the tables of Manila. With its attention to detail and dedication to carefully selected local and imported ingredients, Brasserie Sagana is not only revered by the French community in Manila but also deeply admired by the locals

2. Lartizan Boulangerie Francaise

4.3      ·  French
Taguig, Metro Manila

Lartizan Boulangerie Francaise is an artisanal French bakery that is also owned by Johnlu Koa of French Baker. This place is known for their wide selection of breads, pastries, and teas that are perfect for any time of the day.

3. The French Baker

4.2      ·  Haute French
Manila, Metro Manila

The French Baker has built its success out of its Founder and President Johnlu G. Koa's vision for innovation: to take the idea of the neighborhood bakery to the next level, allowing every Filipino to enjoy freshly-baked, high-quality, and affordable breads.

4. Metronome

4.5      ·  Modern French
Makati, Metro Manila

Opened in 2019, Metronome is a modern French restaurant tucked away in the heart of Manila's business district. It has been named Best New Restaurant in the Philippines by The Philippine Tatler in 2019, one of the Best Food Holiday Destinations (2019) by Mr. Porter, is on the list of 15 New Restaurants to Try (2019) by CNN Travel and continues to earn many praises for its relaxed yet refined approach to fine dining. More recently, Chef Miko was named Chef of the Year in 2021 by Philippine Tatler Dining and in 2022, Metronome was was included in the prestigious 50 Best Discovery guide.

5. Paris Délice

4.3      ·  French
Makati, Metro Manila

Paris Délice is an alternative fast food restaurant that carries authentic French bread, pastries, sandwiches, quiches and salads, all at reasonable prices.

6. Cafe France

3.5      ·  French Cafe
Manila, Metro Manila

CafeFrance Corporation owns and operates a chain of cafes. The Company offers coffee, food products, and breads, including bread crumbs, baguette breads, chocolate croissants, sultana rolls, butter croissants, pastries, savories, rice meals, sandwiches, beverages, and s'choquettes.
